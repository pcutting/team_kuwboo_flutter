Delete Your Kuwboo Account

Last updated: 15 April 2026 · Part of our Privacy Policy

You can delete your Kuwboo account at any time. We offer two options because they suit different situations. Pick the one that fits you.

Option 1 — Deactivate (partial deletion)

Your profile is hidden, notifications stop, you disappear from search and discovery. Your data is kept for 30 days so you can come back by just signing in.

Choose this if: you need a break, you're not sure, or you want the option to reactivate.

  • Other users see "Deleted user" where your name used to appear.
  • Chats and messages you've already sent remain visible to the people you sent them to.
  • Listings, reviews, and posts are hidden.
  • After 30 days without signing back in, your account moves to full deletion automatically.
  • Signing back in within 30 days restores your account exactly as it was.

Option 2 — Delete permanently (full deletion)

Your personal data is erased within 30 days of your request. This cannot be undone after the 30-day grace period.

Choose this if: you're certain you want to leave and you don't plan to return.

  • Your profile, posts, listings, photos, and videos are removed.
  • Your phone number, email, and social sign-in link are purged.
  • Messages you've sent to others remain in the recipient's inbox but are attributed to "Deleted user".
  • A small amount of data may be retained as explained in What we must keep.

How to delete your account — in the Kuwboo app

  1. Open Kuwboo on your phone.
  2. Tap your Profile (bottom-right tab).
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon, top-right of the profile screen).
  4. Scroll to Account.
  5. Tap Delete account.
  6. Choose Deactivate (partial) or Delete permanently (full).
  7. Confirm with the one-time code we send to your phone or email. This confirms it's really you.

Why a grace period?

Two reasons:

  • Accidents happen. People change their minds, or tap the wrong button. A 30-day window lets you recover without losing everything.
  • Safety. Immediate hard-delete would let bad actors erase their evidence and come straight back under a new account. The grace period (combined with ban-evasion detection) makes the platform safer for everyone.

Third-party sign-in (Apple, Google)

If you signed up with Apple or Google, deleting your Kuwboo account does not revoke the linked Apple or Google sign-in on its own. To fully remove the link:

  • Apple: iPhone Settings → Your Name → Sign in with Apple → find Kuwboo → Stop Using Apple ID.
  • Google: myaccount.google.com/permissions → find Kuwboo → Remove access.
